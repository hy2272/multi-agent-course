---
name: email-writer
description: Write targeted emails — launch announcements, follow-ups, re-engagement, or cold outreach. Use when the user needs to write an email to customers, leads, students, or any audience.
---

Ask the user:
1. "What type of email? (launch / follow-up / re-engagement / cold outreach / other)"
2. "Who is the recipient and what do you want them to do after reading?"
3. "Any context about your relationship with them or what they already know?"

Then write the email:

---

**Subject line:** [3 options — one curiosity-driven, one direct, one benefit-led]

**Email body:**

[Opening line that earns the read — specific, not generic]

[2–3 short paragraphs. Each one should advance the reader toward the CTA.]

[One clear CTA — a single link, reply request, or next step. Never two CTAs.]

[Sign-off appropriate to the relationship]

---

**Notes on this email:**
- What's working: [2 things]
- What to watch: [1 risk — e.g. "the opening assumes they remember you, which may not be true"]

**Subject line recommendation:** [Which of the 3 to use and why]

---

Ask: "Want a follow-up version in case they don't reply, or a shorter version for mobile?"
