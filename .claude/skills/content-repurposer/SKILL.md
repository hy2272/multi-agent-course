---
name: content-repurposer
description: Turn one long-form piece into 5 platform-specific formats. Use when the user has an article, post, or talk and wants to distribute it across channels without rewriting everything from scratch.
---

Ask the user to paste their long-form content (or a URL if Brave MCP is available).

Then produce all 5 formats:

---

## Content Repurposing: [Title]

### 1. Tweet thread (Twitter/X)
- Tweet 1: the hook — one punchy sentence that makes people stop scrolling
- Tweets 2–6: one idea per tweet, no filler
- Tweet 7: call to action (link, follow, reply)
Format: numbered, 280 chars max each

### 2. LinkedIn post
150–220 words. Story-led or insight-led. Hook on line 1. Line breaks every sentence. One CTA at the end.

### 3. Newsletter blurb
3–4 sentences. Tease the idea enough to make the reader want the full piece. Ends with a link prompt: "Read the full piece here →"

### 4. Substack Note
50–80 words. Conversational, first-person. Shares one specific thing you learned or noticed. No hard sell.

### 5. TL;DR (for Slack, WhatsApp, or internal share)
3 bullets. What it is, why it matters, what to do with it.

---

Keep each format true to the platform's native style. Don't just shorten — reframe for the context.
