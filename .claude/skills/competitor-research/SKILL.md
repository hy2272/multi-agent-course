---
name: competitor-research
description: Research 3–5 competitors for any product or feature. Returns positioning, pricing, key differentiators, and gaps. Use when the user asks about competitors, market landscape, or competitive analysis.
---

Ask the user: "What product or feature are you researching? Who is it for?"

Then research 3–5 direct competitors using web search. For each competitor return:

**[Competitor Name]**
- **Positioning:** one sentence on how they describe themselves
- **Target customer:** who they're built for
- **Pricing:** tiers and price points if public
- **Key differentiators:** 2–3 things they do well
- **Weaknesses:** 1–2 gaps or complaints from reviews/forums

After all competitors, add a **Gap Analysis** section:
- What no competitor does well
- Where pricing is underserved
- One positioning angle that is unclaimed

Keep each competitor to 6 bullet points max. No padding. End with one sharp question: "Based on this, which gap are you trying to own?"
