---
name: okr-writer
description: Turn a goal statement into well-formed OKRs — 3 Objectives each with 3 measurable Key Results. Use when the user wants to write OKRs, quarterly goals, or structured targets for their team.
---

Ask the user:
1. "What's the big goal or focus area for this period? One sentence is fine."
2. "What timeframe — Q1, H1, full year?"
3. "Is this for a team, a product, a company, or an individual?"

Then produce:

## OKRs — [Team/Product] [Period]

---

**OKR health check:**
- Are the KRs measurable without debate? ✓/✗
- Do they require stretch to hit? ✓/✗
- Are all KRs outputs, not activities? ✓/✗ (Flag any KR that's a task, not an outcome)
- Does each Objective have at least one quality or customer KR? ✓/✗

---

Flag any KR that sounds like a task ("Launch feature X") and rewrite it as an outcome ("Increase X metric by Y").
